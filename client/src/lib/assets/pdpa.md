# Privacy Notice 

Job Applier 3000 (hereinafter referred to as “Data Controller” or "we") is aware of the importance of Personal Data (defined below) protection of Data Controller’s customers and trading and business partners (collectively referred to as “you” or “Data Subject”). The collection, use and/or disclosure (collectively referred to as “Processing” or “Data Processing”) of Personal Data shall act in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act BE 2562 as the Personal Data protection is a social responsibility so as to build trust and strong business relations between Data Controller and its customers and trading and business partners. Data Controller hereby announces this Privacy Notice in order to inform you of your rights and legal duties, as well as terms and conditions concerning the collection, use, or disclosure of your Personal Data.

The term “Personal Data” means any information relating to a person which enables the identification of such person, whether directly or indirectly, but not including the information of deceased persons in particular. 

Job Applier 3000 is a comprehensive B2B2C recruitment platform designed to eliminate fragmented and informal job posting systems for students and alumni within the Department of Computer Engineering, Kasetsart University. Proper operation of the service requires the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Data as described below.

Data Controller may use Personal Data for the following purposes or for other purposes notified at the time of collecting Personal Data or for which Data Subject has given consent after Data Controller has collected such Personal Data. 1. it is to facilitate recruitment activities, including but not limited to enabling Data Subjects to create job applications, share their profiles, resumes, and supporting documents with recruiters or potential employers, and enabling recruiters to review, evaluate, and contact job applicants for hiring purposes; in this regard, such Processing shall occur only as necessary for the recruitment workflow requested or initiated by Data Subject;
1. it is to facilitate recruitment-related communications and transparency from recruiters or companies to Data Subjects, including but not limited to providing job descriptions, company profiles, employment conditions, interview information, supporting documents, and other materials necessary for the job application or hiring process; in this regard, such Processing shall occur only as necessary for the recruitment workflow requested or initiated by Data Subject;
1. it is to enter into a contract or for the performance of a contract between Data Controller and Data Subject or third party for Data Subject’s interests;
1. it is to respond to Data Subject’s enquiry and assist Data Subject;
1. it is to improve the quality of goods, products and services of Data Controller so as to satisfy Data Subject’s demand or needs;
1. it is to survey feedbacks, analyse, research, and gather statistical data for marketing purposes or in order to develop and improve Data Controller’s organisation operations upon the consent of Data Subject;
1. it is for Data Controller’s internal organisation operations and business practices required for Data Controller’s legitimate interests; 
1. it is to inspect, supervise and secure the safety in Data Controller’s sites;
1. it is for Data Controller’s organisation operations, including but not limited to tax withholding;
1. it is required to give or disclose Personal Data to it is required by law to authorised official organisations, including but not limited to the Royal Thai Police Headquarters, the Anti-Money Laundering Office, the Revenue Department and courts;
1. it is to carry out any accounting and financial activities; including but not limited to auditing, debt notification and collection, tax operations and transactions as prescribed by law;
1. It is for Data Controller’s legitimate interests;
1. It is for compliance with law, investigation process, relevant regulations and Data Controller’s legal duties; and
1. it is for other purposes upon the explicit consent from Data Subject.

1. contact information, including but not limited to home address, phone number, social media, personal website and email address;
1. documents, including but not limited to resume, CV (curriculum vitae), transcript, and business registration certificate; and

## Disclosure of Personal Data

Data Controller shall not disclose and transfer Personal Data to third party unless explicit consent is given, or except for where:

1. Data Controller is subject to disclosing or sharing Personal Data with partner, service provider or third party if necessary, in order to fulfil and achieve the purposes contemplated in this Privacy Notice; by doing so, Data Controller shall create a Data Processing Agreement in accordance with the law;
1. Data Controller may disclose or share Personal Data to Data Controller’s affiliates; in this regard, the Processing of Personal Data shall merely be in keeping with the purposes contemplated in this Privacy Notice;
1. it is required to disclose Personal Data by law or legal procedures or to disclose Personal Data to officers, authorities or authorised organisations in order to comply with lawful order or request; and
1. Data Controller may disclose Personal Data to other Data Subjects when such disclosure is necessary for recruitment activities requested or initiated by Data Subject, including but not limited to submitting job applications, sharing resumes, enabling communication related to hiring, providing names, documents, contact information, or other business-related information of authorised representatives involved in the hiring process; such disclosure shall be limited to information required for communication, interview coordination, or evaluation during the recruitment process.

## Data Subject Rights

Data Subject may exercise Data Subject Rights with respect to the law and as contemplated in this Privacy Notice, details as specified below:
1. Right to access and obtain copy of Data Subject’s Personal Data; 
1. Right to have inaccurate Personal Data rectified, or completed if it is incomplete; 
1. Right to Data Portability in case where Data Controller has made such Personal Data publicly accessible in the format readable or commonly used by ways of automatic tools or equipment, and can be used or disclosed by automated methods; 
1. Right to erase, destroy or anonymise Personal Data, should such Personal Data be no longer necessary or unless there are certain circumstances where Data Controller has legal grounds to reject Data Subject’s request; 
1. Right to restrict the use of Personal Data under circumstances where Personal Data is subject to erasure or is no longer necessary; 
1. Right to withdraw consent given by Data Subject; and
1. Right to object the Processing of Personal Data at any time.
Should you have any questions or wish to rectify or erase your Personal Data or to exercise the aforementioned rights or contact Data Controller regarding Personal Data issues or Data Controller’s Personal Data protection practices, please contact Data Controller.

## International Transfers of Personal Data

Data Controller may send or transfer Personal Data to a foreign country for processing and storage. In this regard, Data Controller shall ascertain that the destination country or international organisation that receives such Personal Data shall have adequate data protection standards and measures. 

The above requirement for adequate data protection standards may not apply in the following situations:

1. where the transfer is required for compliance with the law that mandates the Data Controller to send or transfer Personal Data to a foreign country;
1. where the Data Controller has informed the Data Subject and obtained explicit consent for the transfer, in cases where the destination country has inadequate Personal Data protection standards, as determined by the Personal Data Protection Committee; and
1. where the transfer is necessary to prevent or suppress danger to the life, body, or health of the Data Subject or another person when the Data Subject is incapable of giving consent at that time, or where the transfer is necessary for carrying out tasks in the public interest of significant importance.

## Retention Period of Personal Data

Data Controller shall retain and use Personal Data as long as necessary for the purposes of Processing Personal Data contemplated in this Privacy Notice or as prescribed by law, except for where it is necessary to retain such Personal Data for other reasons, including but not limited to acting in accordance with the law, to fulfilling Data Controller’s legal duties and obligations or to inspecting in case where any disputes arise. Under such circumstances, Data Controller may retain Personal Data longer than the abovementioned period.

Upon account deletion or cessation of service usage, the Data Controller shall erase, destroy, or anonymise all Personal Data without undue delay, unless retention is required for legal obligations, dispute resolution, or law-enforcement purposes.
